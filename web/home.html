Nau Mai, Harere Mai / Welcome


The Maori phrase Kai Waho can be translated as outdoor cuisine. It can also describe a master, or teacher, of the ways of the wilderness. For guests at The Haukai Restaurant, on the beautiful tribal lands of the Tuwharetoa iwi, both these meanings merge in an unforgettable and unique cultural, food and wilderness experience.

We look forward welcoming you as our manuhuri (guest) and sharing our knowledge and passion with you.
